Favourite Book

मन में है विश्वास

विश्वास नांगरे पाटील

Book Name:Man Me Hai Vishwas(मन में है विश्वास)

Author Name:Vishwas Nagre Patil
Language– Marathi.


Shri.Vishwas Nangare Patil one of the famous personality in the Department Of Maharashtra Police.Born in a very small village named Kokrud in Kolhapur district, suffered lots o f hardships from day one till date has written stories on his real life in his famous book-MAN MEIN HAIN VISHWAS It is real life based story on life of an IPS Officer Vishwas Nagre Patil .

How many struggles were faced by him in his childhood till date has been described in this book.

the vishwas nangare patil is the idol of most of the people in Maharashtra. Vishwas nangare patil share his own experience with number of people or students which are preparing for ups

In a very simple language he has presented his real life examples.Its not only money matters in our life but all the moral values also play very important role to become successful person ,whoever it may be but self confidence,trust within,dedication,devotion etc will lead you to true success.He born in very small village named Kokrud in Kolhapur district and suffered lots of hardship from day one till now date has written.Studied in Zilla Parushad school and gained such high level achievement to become an IPS Officer .Played a very brave role countering the 26/11 Mumbai attack .This book really inspires the young minds to devote their whole soul in doing any of the hard work with strong power and will really make the Indian police department a grand success.I wish that everyone should read his real life story .

Hats off to this great personality!!!!!!!
